MARVEL ENEMY's

Thanos is a powerful supervillain in the Marvel Universe, first appearing in "Iron Man" #55 in 1973. He is a Titanian Eternal obsessed with death and seeks to gain ultimate power through the Infinity Stones. Thanos aims to balance the universe by eliminating half of its population. He is known for his immense strength, intelligence, and ruthless nature. His character plays a central role in major Marvel storylines, including "The Infinity Gauntlet" and the Marvel Cinematic Universe's "Infinity Saga."

Doctor Strange, a Marvel Comics character, is a former neurosurgeon who becomes the Sorcerer Supreme after a car accident leads him to seek healing and knowledge of the mystic arts. His real name is Dr. Stephen Strange. He protects Earth from magical and mystical threats, using powerful spells, the Eye of Agamotto, and other ancient artifacts. Created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko, he first appeared in "Strange Tales" #110 in 1963. The character is portrayed by Benedict Cumberbatch in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Loki, a character in Marvel Comics, is the Norse God of Mischief and brother to Thor. Known for his trickery and cunning, Loki often shifts between villain and antihero roles. He possesses magical abilities, including shape-shifting and illusion casting. Driven by jealousy and a desire for power, Loki frequently seeks to overthrow Asgard or rule Earth. Despite his villainous nature, he has complex relationships with his family, particularly Thor, adding depth to his character.
